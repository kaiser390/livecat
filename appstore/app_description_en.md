## Description
Turn your iPhone into a professional wireless camera for OBS Studio.

OLiveCam streams high-quality H.264 video over your local Wi-Fi network directly to OBS — no cables, no extra plugins, no watermarks.

SIMPLE SETUP
1. Enter your PC's IP address in the app
2. Add a Media Source in OBS (udp://@:9000)
3. Tap Start — you're live

KEY FEATURES
• 1080p / 720p H.264 hardware encoding
• Ultra-low latency over local Wi-Fi
• Audio streaming support
• Works with OBS Studio on Windows, Mac, and Linux
• No OBS plugins required — standard UDP/MPEG-TS
• Remote zoom control from PC
• Auto-reconnect on connection drops
• Battery and thermal monitoring
• Landscape optimized for streaming

PERFECT FOR
• Live streamers who need a wireless camera
• YouTube / Twitch broadcasters
• Pet cams and home monitoring
• Vloggers and content creators
• Multi-camera OBS setups

PRIVACY FIRST
• Local network only — no cloud, no data collection
• No account required
• No ads, no tracking

Just your iPhone, your Wi-Fi, and OBS. That's it.
