OLiveCam - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Overview

OLiveCam is a camera streaming app that sends video over your local Wi-Fi network to OBS Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No data is sent to any server outside your local network

Network Usage

OLiveCam communicates exclusively over your local Wi-Fi network to send video and audio streams to your computer running OBS Studio. No data is transmitted over the internet by the app.

Camera and Microphone

OLiveCam requires access to your device's camera and microphone to capture video and audio for streaming to OBS. This data is sent only to the IP address you configure in the app settings, on your local network.

Local Network

OLiveCam requires Local Network permission to discover and communicate with your computer on the same Wi-Fi network.

Third-Party Services

OLiveCam does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

OLiveCam does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will post the changes on this page with a new date.
